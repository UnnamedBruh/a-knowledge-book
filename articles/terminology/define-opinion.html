opinion

Noun:
  • a personal belief, judgement or viewpoint not necessarily based on fact or knowledge; a subjective interpretation of a topic or matter. "I don't agree with his opinion"
    • a conclusion or perspective formed from a general understanding, experience or observation. "his opinion about handles is agreeable; they should be placed on top of stairways"
Verb:
  • (nonstandard usage) express an opinion (of). "he is known to opinion literature"
Adjective:
  • (nonstandard spelling) (referring to one's perspective or viewpoint) formed from opinions and open to ideas; open-minded. "she was opinioned, but open to ideas"

opinionate

Verb:
  • (nonstandard spelling & usage) strongly or stubbornly express an opinion (of). "he is widely disrespected due to his desire to opinionate lights"
Adjective:
